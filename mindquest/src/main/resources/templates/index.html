What is MindQuest?

MindQuest is your personal mental wellness companion designed to help you track and improve your mental state. By completing a daily quiz, you receive a personalized percentage score that reflects your current state of mind. Alongside your score, you'll get a tailored tip to help you enhance your well-being.

  • With MindQuest, you can monitor your progress over time and discover strategies to maintain a healthier, happier mind.
  • Start your journey to better mental wellness with MindQuest today!
